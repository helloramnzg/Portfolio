About me
I am a designer who loves to make things, tell stories through them, and build experiences that actually move people.
Manila, Philippines

I'm Rose Anne, but most people call me RA. I'm a web designer and front-end builder based in Manila, and at the core of it all, I'm someone who loves to create.

My path into design wasn't a straight line, but every detour taught me something useful. I started out learning SEO at Bizme Solutions, which taught me early on that how a page is structured matters as much as what's on it. From there I moved into digital content production at Viva Communications, spending years understanding how people consume content, what makes them stop scrolling, and what makes them leave. By the time I moved into design at Accel Scaling, I wasn't starting from zero. I was bringing everything with me.

Once I started designing, I couldn't stop at just the visuals. I wanted to understand how pages were built, how systems connected, how a form submission turned into a follow-up email. One skill led to another until I was owning the whole thing, from the first wireframe to the live build to the automated backend running quietly behind it.

That's the part I love most. Seeing something go from a blank canvas to a fully functioning experience, and knowing I built every layer of it.

Today I work across landing pages, websites, sales funnels, and booking flows. I think of every project as a story with a beginning, a middle, and a very intentional end, designing with UX intent, building on GoHighLevel, Elementor, WordPress, and custom HTML and CSS, and connecting everything through Zapier and API integrations.

Somewhere along the way I realised that the pages I was most proud of weren't just the ones that looked good. They were the ones that anyone could use and the right person couldn't walk away from.
